About ENSTA

Higher institute that provides advanced technical education and applied research, organizing both preparatory training and higher studies in engineering and technology. The school presents core domains of formation such as Sciences & Technologies and Mathematics & Informatics, and offers specialties in areas like Mechanical Energetics, Renewable Energies, Artificial Intelligence, and Systems Security.

Programs & Specialties

ENSTA provides comprehensive training paths from preparatory cycles to advanced engineering specialties, emphasizing internships and industry collaborations.

Masters & Specialties

Sciences & Technologies (ST)

Preparatory Cycle

Comprehensive foundation in sciences and technologies preparing students for national competitive access and orientation toward engineering cycles. Strong emphasis on mathematics, physics, and engineering sciences.

Core Focus Areas:

  • Advanced Mathematics
  • Physics & Engineering Sciences
  • Computer Science Fundamentals
  • Technical Drawing & Design

Mathematics & Informatics (MI)

Preparatory Cycle

Intensive training in mathematics and computer science, building strong analytical and computational skills essential for advanced engineering studies in digital technologies and computer systems.

Core Focus Areas:

  • Advanced Mathematics & Algorithms
  • Programming & Software Engineering
  • Data Structures & Databases
  • Computer Architecture

Mechanical Engineering

Engineering Cycle

Advanced training in mechanical systems design, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and manufacturing processes. Hands-on experience with modern mechanical engineering tools and technologies.

Specializations:

  • Mechanical Design
  • Thermal Engineering
  • Production Methods
  • Materials Engineering

Electrical Engineering

Engineering Cycle

Comprehensive education in electrical systems, power electronics, control systems, and industrial automation. Focus on both theoretical foundations and practical applications.

Specializations:

  • Power Systems
  • Industrial Automation
  • Control Engineering
  • Renewable Energy Systems

Mechanical Energetics

Master Specialization

Advanced studies in energy systems, thermodynamics, and mechanical power generation. Focus on sustainable energy solutions and efficient power systems design.

Key Areas:

  • Thermal Systems Design
  • Energy Conversion
  • Power Plant Engineering
  • Energy Efficiency

Renewable Energy & Storage

Master Specialization

Specialized training in renewable energy technologies, energy storage systems, and sustainable energy management. Emphasis on solar, wind, and emerging renewable technologies.

Key Areas:

  • Solar Energy Systems
  • Wind Power Technology
  • Energy Storage Solutions
  • Smart Grid Integration

Artificial Intelligence & Applications

Master Specialization

Cutting-edge program focusing on AI algorithms, machine learning, computer vision, and intelligent systems. Practical applications in various engineering domains.

Key Areas:

  • Machine Learning
  • Computer Vision
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Intelligent Systems

Systems Security

Master Specialization

Comprehensive training in cybersecurity, network security, and systems protection. Focus on securing industrial systems, networks, and critical infrastructure.

Key Areas:

  • Network Security
  • Cybersecurity
  • Industrial Systems Protection
  • Information Assurance

We have great people!

From robotics to design, computer science to mechanics, our student clubs make campus life active and creative. Click any club to see members, projects, and workshops.

AUDAX Club

The Cultural Club fosters creativity and artistic expression among students. Members engage in diverse activities such as painting, traditional crafts, music, dance, and theater. By organizing exhibitions, workshops, and cultural events, the club promotes cultural awareness and provides students with opportunities to develop their artistic talents and share them with the campus community.

Computer Science Club

The Computer Science Club brings together students passionate about technology, programming, and innovation. Members participate in workshops and projects on web development, artificial intelligence, software engineering, and emerging computer science fields. The club encourages collaboration, organizes coding competitions, and provides opportunities for students to build real-world applications while enhancing their technical skills and knowledge.

Mechanics Club

Working on mechanical projects, product design, production methods, and practical experiments in engineering mechanics.

Automation & Arduino Club

Learning electronics, Arduino, automation projects, and building smart devices for innovation and applied research.

Green & Nature Club

Promoting eco-friendly initiatives, environmental awareness, tree planting, and conservation activities on campus.

ENSTIN Club

A collaborative club bringing together students from robotics, design, computer science, mechanics, and environmental projects to work on multidisciplinary initiatives.
